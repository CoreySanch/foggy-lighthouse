About the Game

Keep the Light is a dark survival-puzzle game where routine becomes a threat. You’re alone, the fog thickens with night, and the lighthouse demands constant care. Ignore the work and the light dies. Push through too long, and your mind starts to slip.

Hands-on lighthouse maintenance

Fuel systems, cleaning, repairs, and tasks that feel physical and grounded.

Survival pressure without a health bar

Hunger and thirst force choices. You feel the consequences, not a UI meter.

Insanity as a slow, eerie build

Isolation and unsettling events raise tension—subtle at first, then undeniable.

Day-by-day survival

Plan your routine, conserve resources, and prepare for nights when the fog closes in.
